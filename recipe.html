Kottu

Kottu is made up of paratha or wheat flour roti, which is cut into small pieces or ribbons.Then on a heated iron sheet or griddle, vegetables and onions are fried. Eggs, cooked meat, or fish are added to fried vegetables and heated for a few minutes. Finally, the pieces of cut paratha are added. These are chopped and mixed by repeated pounding using heavy iron blades/spatula, the sound of which is very distinctive and can usually be heard from a long distance. Depending upon what ingredients are used, the variations are vegetable, egg, chicken, beef, mutton, and fish kottu roti. It is often prepared and served as a fast food dish.

Ingredients

  • paratha or wheat flour roti
  • vegetables
  • onions
  • Eggs
  • cooked meat
  • salt
